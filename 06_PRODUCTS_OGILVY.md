### Category 1: Hand Tools (The Foundation)

**ENGLISH:**
**What They Are:** Wrenches, pliers, screwdrivers, hammers—the tools that always work because they have no batteries to die, no moving parts to break.

**What They Do:** Every job requires them. Good hand tools last decades. Bad ones last weeks.

**Who Needs Them:** Everyone. Shipping, factories, repair shops, utilities.

**GREEK:**
Κλειδιά, τσιμπίδια, κατσαβίδια, σφυριά—τα εργαλεία που λειτουργούν πάντα.

### Category 2: Power Tools (When Speed Matters)

**ENGLISH:**
**What They Are:** Cordless drills, impact drivers, saws, grinders—when one hand tool isn't enough and time is money.

**What They Do:** Cut your job time in half. Milwaukee doesn't make weak drills. Knipex doesn't make weak saws.

**Who Needs Them:** Factories, construction, heavy repair operations.

**GREEK:**

### Category 3: Pneumatic Equipment (High Volume, High Speed)

**ENGLISH:**
**What They Are:** Air compressors, pneumatic drills, impact wrenches—for operations where you're running full tilt.

**What They Do:** Consistent power, no batteries to fail, built for continuous operation.

**Who Needs Them:** Manufacturing plants, high-volume repair shops.

**GREEK:**
Συμπιεστές αέρα, πνευματικά τρυπάνια, κρουστικές δύναμεις—για λειτουργίες που τρέχουν πλήρως.

### Category 4: Safety Equipment (Non-Negotiable)

**ENGLISH:**
**What They Are:** Hard hats, gloves, eye protection, respiratory protection—the stuff that keeps people safe.

**What They Do:** Protect your team. Meet OSHA standards. Reduce accidents and liability.

**Who Needs Them:** Everyone. No exceptions.

**GREEK:**

### Category 5: Maintenance & Cleaning (The Unglamorous Work)

**ENGLISH:**

**GREEK:**
